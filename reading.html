Reading

Here we provide an expansive bibliography of literature on Viking-Age craft

This began with the production of the Craft and Social Networks in Viking Towns volume (Ashby and Sindbaek (eds) 2019). This will be updated periodically, but we make no claim to its being comprehensive or up-to-the-minute.

It should, however, be a good first stop for anyone considering research in the field of Viking-Age craft. Please see Research for relevant new publications.
